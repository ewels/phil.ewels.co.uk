About me

I lead the Genomics Applications Development group at the NGI in Stockholm. The NGI is the largest platform at SciLifeLab - a national centre for biosciences in Sweden. Our group works with the development of new techniques and protocols, both in the lab and in bioinformatics.

My personal interests lie in bioinformatics and epigenetics. I've developed several bioinformatics tools, usually with a focus on quality control and automation of analyses (see below).
